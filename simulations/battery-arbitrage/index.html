Let's begin with the concept of energy storage, then layer complexities of operations and finance.

This series builds intuition for how grid-scale energy storage works. We start with a toy model demonstrating where gross margins come from. Later, we will demonstrate how operations are optimized for the wholesale energy market in deregulated markets like California. Then we will build a project finanace model of a ficticious storage project.
