Battery arbitrage

Part 2 — Optimization with forecasts

Home Simulations

What changes in Part 2?

Part 1 used a simple threshold rule on a single day of prices. Here, we’ll choose the charging and discharging decisions to optimize gross margin, using a price forecast (and later: uncertainty).

Goal

Given a forecast of prices over time, decide how much to charge/discharge each hour to maximize gross margin, while respecting the battery’s physical constraints.

  • Inputs: forecast prices, capacity, power limit, efficiency
  • Decision: charge/discharge schedule (hour-by-hour)
  • Output: profit + state of charge over time

What we’re optimizing (conceptually)

Optimization function: gross margin over time period 

argmax gross margin =  Σ_t ( price_t * discharge_t  -  price_t * charge_t )
subject to state-of-charge, power limits, and efficiency
