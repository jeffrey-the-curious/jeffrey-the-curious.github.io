What happens when score determines survival?

Every generation, agents are randomly paired and play a prisoner's dilemma with 5% signal noise and a random game length. Higher-scoring agents reproduce more. Lower-scoring agents are replaced. Over many generations, the population tilts toward whatever strategies outcompete their neighbors — not just on average, but against whoever they happen to meet.
