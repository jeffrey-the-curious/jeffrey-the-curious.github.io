Spatial evolution

Strategies compete on a grid — cooperation clusters and defectors can be contained

Home Simulations Game Theory

Geography changes everything.

In Part 4, agents were randomly re-paired every generation — cooperation had nowhere to hide from exploitation. Here, each agent interacts only with its 8 neighbors on a toroidal grid. Cooperative strategies can cluster together, earn well in the interior, and push defectors to the margins. The same strategies, a different world, a different outcome.
