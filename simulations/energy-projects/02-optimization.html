Battery arbitrage

Part 2 — Optimization with forecasts

What changes in Part 2?

Part 1 used a simple threshold rule on a single day of prices. Here, we’ll improve the charging and discharging decisions to optimize gross margin, using a price forecast. We will assume perfect information for now (perfect forecasts) and explore uncertainty and model predictive control in the next part.

Goal

Given a forecast of prices over time, decide how much to charge/discharge each hour to maximize gross margin, while respecting the battery’s physical constraints.
